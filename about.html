About me ,In short

I am a wanderer of words and frames, captivated by stories and the art of evoking emotions through images Though my education is rooted in technology, photography is where I pour my soul, crafting moments that speak without uttering a sound. I cherish photographs that reveal their essence with clarity, yet I resist the confines of a single style, choosing instead to experiment and adapt based on the story I wish to tell.

My life dances between Jashore, my serene hometown, and Dhaka, where I am shaping my future through my bachelor’s degree. Beyond needs,I am an explorer delving into whatever sparks my curiosity. Challenges invigorate me, and I find fulfillment in channeling my knowledge toward achieving my aspirations. The rest of who I am is revealed through the things I create and the paths I dare to tread.
